About PSL

PROMETHEUS SPORTS
LEAGUE

Prometheus Sports League is built around a simple belief: sport is not just an activity. It is a pathway to character, discipline, resilience and leadership.

As the sports format within Noida Talent Hunt, PSL gives young athletes a structured platform to compete, grow and experience professional sporting culture. With Prometheus School and Prometheus Sports Academy behind it, the league brings together training infrastructure, experienced mentors and a clear ambition to prepare future champions for bigger stages.

Sporting CultureCreated to build confidence, resilience and future champions through regular competition.
Olympic MindsetRooted in the idea that athletes should be prepared for the discipline of the Olympic stage.
ScaleThe first edition brought 900+ participants across 6 sports, with schools joining from across India.
Scholarship PathwayLinked to Prometheus sports scholarships for national-level performers and rising sports stars.
